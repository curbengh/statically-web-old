With screenshot service, you can capture web-pages from the internet by its URL and then turn it into a PNG or JPG image. No need to open the web browser, turn on browser extension and do Ctrl + Shift + V. Instead of doing that manually, simply paste a web-page URL to the text field above to get the screenshot URL.
